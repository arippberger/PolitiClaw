# Capabilities

PolitiClaw is organized around a set of focused OpenClaw tools instead of one large catch-all command surface.

## Legislation and votes

- Search federal bills by chamber, congress, issue area, update window, or title fragment.
- Fetch bill details and summaries from Congress.gov-backed data.
- Ingest House roll-call vote data and use it as structured scoring input.
- Monitor upcoming hearings and House activity with recurring OpenClaw jobs.

## Representatives and alignment

- Resolve representatives from a saved address.
- Score representatives against declared issue stances.
- Generate periodic representative reports from the stored alignment data.
- Compare challengers and incumbents with FEC-backed finance summaries.

## Ballot workflows

- Fetch a personalized ballot when Google Civic is configured.
- Explain what a contest or ballot measure decides in plain language.
- Research candidates with structured finance data and source caveats.

## Monitoring

- Install recurring weekly summaries and watch jobs through the monitoring setup tool.
- Pause and resume the managed jobs without deleting the rest of a user's OpenClaw cron setup.
- Keep routine monitoring quiet when nothing materially changed.

## What it does not try to be

- It is not a multi-user civic platform.
- It does not send outreach on your behalf.
- It does not turn low-confidence narrative research into fake precision.
